About

I build the unglamorous parts — the CI templates, the Terraform modules, the dashboards nobody looks at until something breaks — and I obsess over getting them right.

Over the last ten years I've moved from administering RHEL boxes in India to architecting multi-cloud GitOps platforms in United States. The thread connecting all of it: a belief that good infrastructure is legible, testable, and quietly reliable. My pipelines fail loudly, my deploys succeed quietly, and my dashboards tell the truth.

Now I'm pointing that discipline at machine learning. Because an MLOps pipeline is still, at the end of the day, a pipeline — and I've been perfecting those for a decade.
